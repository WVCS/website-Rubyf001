Extra Facts About Endangered Species in Canada

In Canada, there are over eight-hundred known species which are threatened, a special concern, or endangered.

The most endangered mammal in Canada is the Atlantic Grey Whale.

Habitat loss is one of the greatest known threats to all species in Canada
