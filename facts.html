Facts About Cats

You should know at least 5 new facts about cats after reading this article!


- Cats are believed to be the only mammals who don’t taste sweetness.

- Cats are nearsighted, but their night vision is much better than humans night vision.

- Cats can jump up to six times their length.

- Cats have 230 bones, while humans only have 206.

- Cats have collarbones which don’t connect to their other bones because these bones are buried in their shoulder muscles.
